PrettyEarth

Download for macOS

Our planet is a beautiful place.
The Google Chrome Earth View extension by Google is a wonderful way to deliver stunning satellite images to us every time we open a new tab, inspiring us on our next web adventure.
But why stop there?
Why don't get impressed by the wonders of the earth when gazing at this more important rectangle on our screen, our wallpaper.

PrettyEarth now grants you the opportunity to bring our blue marble to your desktop.
